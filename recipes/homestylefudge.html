Homestyle Fudge

Nothing ties up the night with a bow better than Grandma Judy's chocolate fudge. This recipe will have you scrambling back to the plate for more and more. It's easy to make, but not easy to keep around. Enjoy this soft, warm fudge for dessert after a long day at the North Pole.

Here's what you'll need

  • 3 cups of white sugar
  • 12 oz or 2 cups of semi sweet chocolate chips
  • 2/3 cup evaporated milk
  • 3/4 butter
  • 1 7oz jar of marshmallow cream
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of chopped nuts (optional)

Here's how to make it

  1. Combine sugar, butter, and milk with wooden spoon until butter is fully melted.
  2. Bring to full boil, and heat for 5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and add chocolate chips, marshmallow cream, and vanilla extract.
  4. Let stand and cool before serving.
